Bolognese

Description

Bolognese takes a long time to cook but it is pretty simple in it's set up.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef/Pork (both if possible)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • One large Onion
  • Two large Carrots
  • Three Celery Stocks
  • Garlic Cloves (to taste)
  • 6oz Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine
  • Parsley
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop up the Onion, Garlic, Carrot and Celery into small pieces. Place in a large pot coated in a heavy amount of olive oil and let simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Once a light brown frond starts to form at the bottom of the pot, add the meat. Mix well with the veg and let simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes.
  3. Once the frond starts turning a darker brown and the liquid of all the veg and meat is gone, add the tomato paste. Mix well and repeat the process until there is a good amount of frond at the bottom of the pot.
  4. Add a decent amount of wine (at least half a bottle) to deglaze the frond.
  5. Add enough water so that everything is submerged. Add the bay leaves and thyme, mix well and let cook for 3-5 hours. Make sure to stir every 15-20 minutes. Add salt and pepper as needed. If liquid is running low, add more water.
  6. Serve with pasta of your choice and add chopped up parsley.
