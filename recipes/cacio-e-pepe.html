Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e Pepe is incredibly simple but hard to get right. Done well, this is a delicious, cheesy sauce with a slight kick.

Ingredients

  • Pecorino Romano
  • Black Peppercorns
  • Bucatini or Spaghetti
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, placea good amount of peppercorns and heat up for about 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool down.
  2. In a pot, boil water and cook pasta a couple minutes less than stated on package. Season lightly witht salt.
  3. Grate a lot of Pecorino. Like more than you think. Just grate a ton
  4. Take the peppercorns and grind them up with a mortar and pestel. You can also just put them in a pepper mill and grind them there.
  5. Take a ladel and scoop out a decent amount of the pasta water after the pasta has been cooking for a few minutes
  6. Once the pasta is finished, drain and let sit for a minute or two
  7. Take the pecorino, place in a sauce pan and slowly start adding small amounts of the pasta water you had set aside. Mix well until you start to get a creamy sauce.
  8. Add the peppercorns and pasta to the sauce and mix vigorously so that all of the pasta gets coated in the sauce. If needed add a bit more pasta water.
