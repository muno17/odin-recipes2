Aglio e Olio

Description

Aglio e Olio is a delicious and simple recipe. What it lacks for ingredient quantity it more than makes up for in taste.

Done correctly, this can be a fast go-to meal when you're low on time.

Ingredients

  • Garlic Cloves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Peperoncino
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. In a pot, boil water and cook spaghetti a couple minutes less than stated on package. Season generously with salt.
  2. Thinly slice the garlic, as thin as you can possibly go.
  3. In a sauce pan, heat up a generous amount of olive oil on medium and add the garlic.
  4. After a couple of minutes, add peperoncino to taste.
  5. After garlic has been cooking for 4 minutes, take a ladel and add a couple of scoops of pasta water into the sauce pan. Stir to mix thoroughly.
  6. When pasta is finished cooking, add directly to sauce pan and finish cooking in there for a couple of minutes. Stir to make sure pasta is sufficiently coated with sauce.
  7. When pasta is al dente, remove and sprinkle with parsley and eat away!
